## Before you start

<Warning>
Before you start, you need a [Checkout Portal account](https://portal.dibspayment.eu). Please refer to the guide:
- How to [create a test account for Checkout](/create-a-checkout-portal-account),
</Warning>

## Overview

Our JTL plugin is the perfect extension that enables you to use the Checkout to its full potential for your JTL store. Checkout supports most popular payment methods.

<Info>
You can see below all the payment methods offered by Checkout. This list refers to all markets. 

Depending on your country or region, the list may vary. If you are uncertain about a specific payment method and whether it is available in your country or region, please contact [Support](https://developer.nexigroup.com/nexi-checkout/en-EU/support/) for more information.
</Info>
