## Overview

The *Settings* section of the Checkout demo store includes the following functionalities:

- [Payment](/payment)
- [Checkout & Merchant](/checkout-merchant)
- [Links](/links)
- [Tools](/settings)

Each of these setting operations can be toggled for your needs and used for testing purposes inside the Checkout demo store.
