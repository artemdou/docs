## Before you start

<Info>
An additional agreement is required for accepting PayPal. Please contact our Customer Service to get that process started.
</Info>

## Overview

Checkout has an integration to PayPal which enables your consumers to pay via their PayPal account.

Payments with PayPal will be processed with the chosen card connected to what the consumer has registered with their PayPal account. You can check the authorization timeframes from this table [here](/payment-methods/#build-authorization-times-for-charge-cancel-and-refund).

## User Journey & Payment Flow

This following section will describe the user journey and payment flow for a consumer:

1. When your consumer chooses PayPal as a payment method in the checkout, they will be prompted to:
   -  Accept terms and conditions
2. Click on Pay.
3. From here, they will be redirected to the PayPal page in a pop-up window.
4. Log in with their credentials.
6. Approve the payment in the pop-up window.
7. From here, they will be redirected back to the checkout.
