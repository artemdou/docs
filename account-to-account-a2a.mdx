## Overview

Account-to-account (also known as A2A) payments allow to transfer funds directly from a bank account. 

Checkout supports the following account-to-account payments:

- [SEPA Direct Debit](/sepa-direct-debit)
- [Swish](/swish)
- [Trustly](/trustly)
