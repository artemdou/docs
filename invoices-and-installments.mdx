## Overview

Invoices and Installments offer your consumers more flexibility for their payments. 

Checkout supports:

- [Riverty](/riverty)
- [RatePay Invoice](/ratepay-invoice)

<Info>
The payment reservation is usually valid up to 30 days, you must charge the payment within this time frame
</Info>
