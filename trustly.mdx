## Overview

Checkout has an integration to Trustly which enables your consumers to pay via their Trustly account.

## User Journey

This following section will describe the user journey for a consumer:

1. Consumers chooses “Trustly” on the payment selection page.
2. Consumers are asked to choose the bank.
3. Consumers have the option to save payment details for future purchases.
4. Consumers gets redirected to the bank page (login to their account).
5. After successful login, the consumer is redirected back to Trustly to confirm their payment.
6. They will see their payment details, before authorizing the payment using SCA.
7. Now the payment is complete and the customer is directed bank to your shop.
