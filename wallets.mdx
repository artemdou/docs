## Overview

Checkout partners with other payment providers to allow the integration of digital wallets into the Checkout payment selection page. 

A digital wallet enables your customers to pay without sharing their funding source details.

Checkout supports the following wallets:

- [MobilePay](/mobilepay)
- [Vipps](/vipps)
- [PayPal](/paypal)
- [Apple Pay](/apple-pay)
- [Klarna](/klarna)
- [Google Pay](/google-pay)
